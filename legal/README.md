# Legal & Compliance Documents

This folder contains legal agreements and compliance documentation for MyFoodFit (Food Signal Ltd).

## Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)

The `dpas/` folder contains Data Processing Agreements with our sub-processors. These documents:

- Establish GDPR-compliant data processing terms with third-party services
- Document the legal basis for international data transfers (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses)
- Should be retained for the duration of the service relationship plus 6 years
